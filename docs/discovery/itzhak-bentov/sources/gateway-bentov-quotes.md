## Centrality note

Bentov is more than a stray citation in the Gateway report. McDonnell uses Bentov early to frame the "physical aspects" of Gateway, then returns to his model for the standing-wave/Kundalini mechanism, heart-aorta resonance, brain stimulation, holographic analogy, and later cosmological speculation. That said, Bentov is not the only pillar: Monroe/Hemi-Sync supplies the operational technique, Stone/Sannella/Pribram/Ferguson/physics sources supply other parts of the scaffolding, and the report's conclusions are McDonnell's synthesis rather than Bentov's text alone.
