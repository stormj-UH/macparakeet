# Negative findings (things checked that came up empty)

Recording these so nobody re-runs the same dead ends, and because a few are
informative in their own right.

## Rex Research — no Bentov entry (checked 2026-07-09)

Rex Research (rexresearch.com + the rexresearch1.com "Library Annex") archives
suppressed / dormant / anomalous technology — Tesla, Rife, Keely, cold fusion,
free energy, radionics. Checked the main index (`rexresearch.com/1index.html`),
the Annex index, and general web search: **no dedicated Bentov page exists.**

Interpretation: this is mild *counter*-evidence to the "suppressed genius" framing.
Bentov's inventions were conventional, well-documented medical devices with public
patents and a clear corporate lineage (Medi-Tech → Boston Scientific), and his
consciousness work was mainstream-published and openly cited by the US Army. None
of it fits the "suppressed invention" pattern Rex Research curates. His work wasn't
buried; it was absorbed (devices) or simply not empirically validated (consciousness).
