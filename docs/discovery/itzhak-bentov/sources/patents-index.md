## Colorful invention claims checked against patents

- **Diet spaghetti:** no US patent in this set substantiates the diet/dietary spaghetti claim. It remains a colorful secondary-source claim unless a food-process patent, company record, article, or memoir page is located.
- **EKG electrode:** no US patent in this set clearly substantiates a surface EKG-electrode claim. US 3,474,791 is an implantable heart-stimulating/pacer electrode, not an EKG recording electrode.
- **Pacemaker lead:** US 3,474,791 substantiates this in substance. It describes an implantable electrode for conducting heart-stimulating current from a supply to heart muscle.
- **Automobile brake shoes:** no US patent in this set substantiates a brake-shoe invention. US 3,119,283 is assigned to General Motors and is automotive-adjacent power transmission, but it is not a brake-shoe patent.
